Android 101

Android 101 is for you who want to learn about the Android platform, but have not yet gotten started. We provide an introduction to several Android components, in addition to network communication, so you will be able to develop applications on your own.

This course consists of a theoretical and a practical part. In the practical part you will develop a Twitter-like application that communicates with a RESTful backend.
